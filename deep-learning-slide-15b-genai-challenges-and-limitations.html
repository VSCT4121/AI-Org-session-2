Challenges & Limitations of GenAI

Key Risks to Consider

Hallucinations

GenAI can confidently generate false or fabricated information, posing risks in high-stakes domains like healthcare, law, and finance.

Bias & Fairness

Models trained on biased data can perpetuate or amplify societal biases, leading to unfair or discriminatory outputs.

High Cost & Resources

Training and running large models demands massive compute, energy, and financial investment, limiting accessibility.

Privacy & Security

Risk of exposing sensitive training data or enabling misuse for phishing, deepfakes, and social engineering attacks.
