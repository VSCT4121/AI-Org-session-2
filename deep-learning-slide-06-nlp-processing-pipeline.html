NLP Processing Pipeline

From raw text to meaningful insights: The 5-stage workflow

Text
Preprocessing

Cleaning raw data: Tokenization, lowercasing, punctuation removal.

In:

Out:

["hello", "world"]

2

Normalization

Standardizing words: Stemming, Lemmatization, Stopword removal.

In:

["running", "ran"]

Out:

3

Feature
Extraction

Converting text to numbers: Bag of Words, TF-IDF, Embeddings.

Out:

[0.24, 0.91, ...]

4

Model
Processing

Applying AI algorithms: Classification, NER, Sentiment Analysis.

5

Post-
processing

Refining output: Formatting, ranking, aggregation.
