Discriminative vs Generative
Discriminative
Learns decision boundary P(Y|X). Maps input to a label or class.
Goal: Classify / Predict
P(cat | image) = 0.97
e.g. ResNet, BERT, SVM
Generative
Learns joint distribution P(X). Can sample entirely new data.
Goal: Create / Generate
X ~ P(image | "a cat")
e.g. GPT-4, Stable Diffusion
