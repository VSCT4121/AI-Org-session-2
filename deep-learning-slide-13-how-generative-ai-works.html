How Generative AI Works

From learning patterns to creating novel content

Architecture

Training Phase

Pattern Recognition & Optimization

01

Massive datasets collected (text, images, code, etc.)

02

Model learns patterns, structures, and relationships

03

Billions of parameters optimized through backpropagation

04

Model captures statistical distributions of training data

Generation Phase

Inference & Creation

01

User provides prompt or seed input context

02

Model processes input through pre-trained neural networks

03

Samples from learned probability distributions

04

Generates new content token-by-token or pixel-by-pixel
