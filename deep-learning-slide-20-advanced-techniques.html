Advanced Techniques

Sophisticated strategies to optimize LLM performance and reliability

01

Chain of
Thought

Ask AI to "think step-by-step" for complex reasoning tasks to improve logic and accuracy.

02

Few-Shot
Learning

Provide 2-3 examples of desired input-output pairs to guide the model's pattern recognition.

03

Constraints &
Guardrails

Specify exactly what NOT to include or set strict length limits to control output scope.

04

Iterative
Refinement

Build on previous responses with specific follow-up prompts to polish and perfect the output.

05

Temperature
Control

Adjust creativity vs. consistency in responses. Low for facts, high for creative writing.

06

Persona
Consistency

Maintain the same assigned role throughout the entire conversation for uniform tone.

Remember: Great prompts are clear, specific, and give the AI enough context to understand your goals.
